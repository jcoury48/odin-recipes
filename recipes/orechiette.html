Orechiette

Description

Classic Puglian pasta dish with balanced flavors in a quick weeknight dish

Ingredients

  • 1/4 C extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 shallot minced
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 15oz can small white navy beans
  • 1 pound broccoli rabe, trimmed and cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 pound orechiette
  • 2 ounces Parmesan cheese, grated (1cup)

Directions

  1. Heat oil in nonstick skillet over medium heat until shimmering.
  2. Add shallot until softened; stir in garlic and pepper flakes; cook until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  3. Stir in beans and cook about 2 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, bring 4 quarts of water to a boil; Add broccoli rabe and 1 tablespoon salt and cook about 2 minutes.
  5. Using slotted spoon, transfer broccoli to bean mixture.
  6. Return water to a boil, add pasta and cook until al dente.
  7. Reserve 1 cup pasta liquid, drain and return past to pot.
  8. Add bean-broccoli mixture, Paremasan and 1/3 cup reserved cooking water and toss to combine. Serve.
