Spaghetti al Limone

Description

A few high-quality ingredients are combined to make a delightful Italian dish

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp grated lemon zest plus 1/3 C lemon juice (2 lemons)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced to paste
  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, grated (1 C)
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 6 T shredded fresh basil
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk oil, lemon zest and juice, garlic, 1/2 tsp of salt, and 14 tsp pepper together in a small bowl, then stir in parmesan cheese.
  2. Meanwhile bring water to boil and add 1 T salt. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  3. Reserve some water, drain pasta and toss with oil mixure and basil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and adjust consistency with reserved waters. Serve.
