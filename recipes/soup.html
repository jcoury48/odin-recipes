Tortilla Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 t olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 T tomato paste
  • 1 tsp chile powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chipotle
  • 1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 4 C chicken broth
  • 1 15oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 15oz can whole kernel corn
  • 1 15oz can black beans
  • 2 C crushed tortilla chips
  • 1 diced avocado
  • lime wedges
