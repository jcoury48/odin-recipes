Tortilla Soup

Description

A classic spicy soup for a fall or winter day. It can be made ahead and refrigerated up to three days or frozen up to three months.

Ingredients

  • 1 t olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 T tomato paste
  • 1 tsp chile powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chipotle
  • 1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 4 C chicken broth
  • 1 15oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 15oz can whole kernel corn
  • 1 15oz can black beans
  • 2 C crushed tortilla chips
  • 1 diced avocado
  • lime wedges

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic to pan; saute for 5 miutes or until tender.
  3. Add tomatoe paste and next 5 ingredients (through salt).
  4. Add broth and next 3ingredients (Through beans); bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to medium, simmer for 15 - 30min.
  6. Remove from heat. Top with torilla chips and avocado; serve with lime wedges.
