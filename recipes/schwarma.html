Chicken Schwarma

Ingredients:

Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 C olive oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 4 cloves of garlic

Garlic Sauce

  • 1/2 C vegetable oil
  • 1/3 C lemon juice
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp salt
