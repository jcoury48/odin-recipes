Chicken Schwarma

Description

Ingredients:

Chicken

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 C olive oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large onion, sliced

Garlic Sauce

  • 1/2 C vegetable oil
  • 1/3 C lemon juice
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp salt

Wraps

  • hummus
  • garlic sauce
  • pita wraps

Directions:

Chicken

  1. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, cumin, black pepper, salt, pepper flakes, turmeric, cinnamon, and garlic.
  2. Toss chicken in mixture; cover and marinate for at least an hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Add onion to to chicken mixture and toss.
  4. Add to large baking dish and bake for 40 - 45minuts; until browned and crisped.
  5. Let chicken rest about 15 minutes, then chop into pieces.

Garlic Sauce

  1. Add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth (1 -2 minutes).
  2. This will make about 1 1/2 cups of sauce.

Chicken Schwarma Wraps

  1. Warm up wraps for about 30 seconds, spread hummus over, add chicken, and drizzle with garlic sauce. Serve
