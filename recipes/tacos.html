Tacos

Description

A quick, flavoful take on chicken tacos for busy weekday dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 T cumin
  • 1 T vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • corn tortillas, warmed
  • 2 avocados, sliced for serving

Directions

  1. Prepare grill with medium-high heat
  2. Mix garlic, cumin, vegetable oil, salt and pepper in medium size bowl. Toss chicken an onion in mixture.
  3. Grill chicken and onion until done and lightly charred, about 4 minutes per side.
  4. Let chicken rest 5 minutes before slicing.
  5. Serve with tortillas, avoacado, lime, and a salsa of your choosin.
